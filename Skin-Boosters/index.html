Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about skin booster treatments.

Most patients benefit from 2 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart for optimal results. Profhilo is typically administered as a course of 2 treatments. Maintenance sessions are recommended every 6-12 months.

Minimal downtime. You may experience slight redness, swelling, or tiny bumps at injection sites which typically resolve within 24-48 hours. Most patients return to normal activities immediately after treatment.

Initial hydration effects are noticeable within days. Collagen stimulation begins at 2-4 weeks, with optimal results visible at 4-8 weeks. Full skin remodelling continues for up to 3 months post-treatment.

Ideal for patients aged 25-65 with concerns about skin hydration, texture, fine lines, and overall skin quality. Not suitable for pregnant or breastfeeding women, or those with active skin infections.
