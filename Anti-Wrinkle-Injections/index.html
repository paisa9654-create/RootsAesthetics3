Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about anti-wrinkle injections answered.

Results typically last 3-4 months. With regular maintenance treatments, some patients find the effects can last longer as muscles become trained to relax. Dr. Mehta will create a personalised maintenance schedule based on your response to treatment.

Most patients experience minimal discomfort, often described as a quick pinch. Dr. Mehta uses ultra-fine needles and can apply topical numbing cream if requested. The entire process takes only 15-30 minutes, and any discomfort subsides immediately after treatment.

Initial effects can be seen within 3-5 days, with full results visible after 10-14 days. The gradual onset ensures natural-looking changes. Dr. Mehta schedules a complimentary 2-week follow-up to assess your results and make any minor adjustments if needed.

Common temporary side effects include mild redness, swelling, or bruising at injection sites, which typically resolve within hours to a few days. Serious complications are extremely rare when treated by a qualified medical professional like Dr. Mehta.

Anti-wrinkle injections are not recommended for pregnant or breastfeeding women, individuals with certain neurological conditions, or those with allergies to any ingredients. During your consultation, Dr. Mehta will conduct a thorough medical assessment to ensure treatment is safe for you.
